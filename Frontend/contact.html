Frequently Asked Questions

What's your shipping policy?

We offer complimentary worldwide shipping on orders over €200. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days.

Do you offer returns?

Yes, we provide free returns within 30 days of purchase. All items must be unworn and in original condition.

How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Use it to monitor your delivery status.

Do you have physical stores?

Currently, we operate exclusively online. However, feel free to schedule a private shopping appointment.

How do I care for my Lavitúr pieces?

Each item includes detailed care instructions. Most pieces are dry-clean only to maintain the premium materials.

Can I request custom sizing?

Absolutely. Contact our team at support@lavitur.com to discuss custom sizing options and pricing.
